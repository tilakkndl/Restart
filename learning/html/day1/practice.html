Class 1

Science

Science is the study of the natural world through observation and experimentation. It helps us understand how things work and why they happen.

Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It is used in everyday life for counting, measuring, and problem-solving.

Class 2

History

History is the study of past events and people. It helps us learn about different cultures and how societies have changed over time.

Geography

Geography is the study of the Earth's landscapes, environments, and the relationships between people and their surroundings.
